About ParkWash

ParkWash was born out of a simple frustration: finding time to properly clean a car while balancing work, studies, and a busy schedule. As a student-entrepreneur, I noticed that company cars often sit parked for hours every day unused, yet perfectly available. That sparked the idea: clean vehicles while people work, without disrupting their day.

ParkWash delivers discreet, on-site vehicle cleaning for companies, directly in underground or private parking facilities. Because many of these locations don’t allow traditional washing methods, we deliberately work with professional dry and low-water cleaning techniques. This allows us to clean vehicles safely, responsibly, and without leaving water, residue, or mess behind.

Our work goes beyond standard company cars. We regularly care for executive vehicles, fleet cars, and also more sensitive vehicles such as oldtimers and pre-war classics, where attention, product choice, and technique matter. In selected cases, we also work with private clients, offering the same professional service at their home.

Sustainability isn’t a slogan for us it’s a practical decision. Our methods drastically reduce water usage while still delivering visible, high-quality results.

Every client and every vehicle is different. That’s why ParkWash offers flexible service options, allowing you to choose the level of detailing that fits your vehicle, expectations, and budget. From efficient maintenance cleans to more in-depth treatments, you stay in control.
